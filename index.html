F SOCIETY

"F Society" is a fictional hacker group known for its activities in the realm of cybersecurity and activism against perceived injustices in society. Led by a core group of individuals, F Society operates clandestinely with the aim of challenging established power structures, particularly those held by large corporations and financial institutions. The group's actions often involve hacking and other forms of technological disruption to promote social change and hold powerful entities accountable for their actions. F Society has become a symbol of resistance within popular culture, representing the potential for grassroots movements to effect meaningful societal transformation.

E CORP

This multinational corporation holds a broad spectrum of industries, actively participating in sectors like finance, technology, and energy. In the series, E Corp is often referred to as "Evil Corp," emphasizing its vast power and the evil it represents for most characters. E Corp becomes the target of characters such as Elliott Alderson and fsociety because they believe weakening the company's power is a path toward social justice. E Corp's presence is central to societal, economic, and political dynamics throughout the series, playing a significant role in the progression of the story.

MR. ROBOT

Elliot's father is involved in abusive and illegal activities that deeply affect his life. Elliot's relationship with his father has a major impact on Elliot's personality and mental state. His father's presence causes Elliot to question his relationship with his own identity and perception of reality.

ELLIOT

Elliot struggles with serious mental issues such as social anxiety and depression. Additionally, he experiences Dissociative Identity Disorder and establishes a relationship with a different alter ego named "Mr. Robot". Elliot also appears as an activist against societal injustices and the unfairness of the system.
